Octopuses and You is a free, online Wikipedia dedicated to providing information about octopuses to everyone and anyone. Here at Octopuses and You, we provide up-to-date news about the octopus world, information about different species, and fundraising for conservation efforts. Octopuses and You has raised $12.42 to date, all of which has been donated in the interest of keeping the noble octopus around for years to come.
